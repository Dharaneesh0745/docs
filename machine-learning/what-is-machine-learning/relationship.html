The AI Family Tree

Understanding the subsets of Artificial Intelligence.

🧠 Artificial Intelligence

The broad concept of machines being able to carry out tasks in a way that mimics human intelligence and logic.

⚙️ Machine Learning

A subset of AI. Instead of writing explicit rules, we feed data to algorithms so they can learn patterns automatically.

🕸️ Deep Learning

A specialized subset of ML. It uses complex, multi-layered "Artificial Neural Networks" inspired by the human brain to process vast amounts of unstructured data.
